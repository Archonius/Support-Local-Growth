How it works

From questionnaire to live site in five steps

  1. Tell us what you need

    Fill in a detailed questionnaire covering your content, pages and look & feel.

  2. Lock in your price

    Pay the flat £250 build fee. That’s the entire cost of getting built and live.

  3. Choose your domain

    Pick your domain name — we register and connect it, or connect one you already own.

  4. We build it

    Your site goes live within days, built to your questionnaire answers.

  5. Fine-tune it

    A revision period follows launch, so wording and details can be adjusted.
