Who are we?

It started by watching local businesses disappear.

Support Local Growth wasn’t built in a boardroom. It came from watching a home town change, one empty shopfront at a time.

The founder grew up watching the shops on his local high street close, one after another. The butcher, the hardware store, the family run café: businesses that had been trading for decades, priced out by rising rents and undercut by chains that could absorb costs they never could. Empty units sat boarded up for months, sometimes years, while new retail parks and chain outlets opened just outside town, pulling footfall away for good.

None of it was because the local businesses were doing anything wrong. They knew their customers, they knew their trade, and they cared about the town.

What they didn’t have was the marketing budget, or the online presence, that let bigger competitors win the fight before it even started. Watching a market town lose its identity to make way for the same handful of national outlets felt like watching something worth saving get pushed aside without a fight.

That’s where the idea for Support Local Growth came from. If independent businesses couldn’t match a national chain’s marketing spend, they at least deserved a fair chance to be found online. So a streamlined, repeatable process was built: one that could deliver a genuinely professional website and search visibility at a price a small local business could actually afford, without cutting corners on quality.

More than a nice-looking page

What a website needs to actually be found

Most small business owners aren’t web developers, and there’s no reason they should be. But a website is far more than what it looks like. Search visibility, page speed, secure connections, and content built in a way search engines can actually read all happen behind the scenes, and all of it decides whether a business gets found online or stays invisible next to competitors with a dedicated marketing team.

That’s the part most small businesses never see. It’s exactly the part we take care of for them.

  • Search engine optimisation built in from the start
  • Fast, lightweight pages that load quickly
  • Secure HTTPS connections as standard
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Content structured the way search engines read it
  • No hidden gaps that leave a business invisible online

Proudly English

A wholly English team, no exceptions

Every part of Support Local Growth, from the first conversation to the finished website, is handled by an English team based in the UK. No outsourcing, no offshore call centres, no handing your project to a subcontractor you’ve never spoken to.

Just people who understand the UK high street, because they’ve watched it struggle firsthand.

  • Direct contact with the people building your site
  • Built by people who understand UK high streets
