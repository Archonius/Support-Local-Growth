Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026.

Who we are

Support Local Growth (“we”, “us”) provides subsidised website design and hosting for independent UK small businesses. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and what your rights are.

What we collect

When you fill in our build form, contact form, or otherwise get in touch, we may collect:

  • Your name, business name, email address and phone number
  • Details about your business (type, description, ownership)
  • Website content preferences: pages, features, colour scheme, style, domain name
  • Payment-related information processed directly by Stripe (we do not see or store your card details)
  • Any other information you choose to send us by email or through our forms

How we use it

We use your information to:

  • Assess eligibility for the subsidised scheme
  • Design, build and deliver your website
  • Set up and manage hosting, domain and billing where applicable
  • Communicate with you about your order, your site, or support requests
  • Meet our legal and accounting obligations

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for advertising or marketing to third parties.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of third-party services to run this scheme, each of whom processes data on our behalf:

  • FormSubmit — delivers form and contact submissions to our email inbox
  • Stripe — processes payments and subscription billing; Stripe handles your card details directly and we never see or store them

We don’t share your information with anyone else except where required by law.

Cookies

This website does not itself use analytics, advertising or tracking cookies. It sets no non-essential cookies. If you proceed to payment, Stripe’s checkout pages may set their own cookies under Stripe’s own privacy and cookie policy, which is outside our control and governed by their terms.

How long we keep it

We retain your information for as long as needed to deliver your website and hosting, meet accounting and tax obligations (typically up to six years), and resolve any disputes. After that, we delete or anonymise it.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, object to or restrict our use of it, and request a copy in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) if you believe we haven’t handled your data properly.

Contact us

For any privacy questions or requests, email hello@supportlocalgrowth.org.
