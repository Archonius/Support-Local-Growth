Standard & Pro hosting includes

What that monthly fee actually covers

  • Secure website hosting
  • SSL security, kept current
  • Domain & DNS management
  • Technical support
  • Minor website updates
  • Ongoing website care
What counts as a “minor update”? Text changes, contact details, opening hours, prices and swapping an existing photo are included, made at a reasonable frequency. New photography, new pages or sections, layout or design changes, and very frequent day-to-day edits fall outside minor updates and are quoted separately — see the FAQ for the full breakdown.
